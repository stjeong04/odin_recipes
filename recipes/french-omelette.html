French Omelette

This is a delicious French omelette made with eggs and cheese that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh herbs
  • 1/4 cup of chopped mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup of chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped ham
  • 1/4 cup of chopped bacon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together with the milk.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, ham, and bacon to the skillet and cook until they are soft, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook until the edges start to set, about 2 minutes.
  5. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges of the omelette towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the cheese and herbs over the omelette.
  7. Continue to cook the omelette until the eggs are set but still slightly runny in the center, about 2 minutes.
  8. Using a spatula, fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate.
