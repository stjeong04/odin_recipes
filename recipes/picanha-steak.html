Picanha Steak

This is a delicious picanha steak that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of picanha steak
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/8 cup of chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and chili flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the picanha steak and cook until it is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the white wine and cook until reduced by half, about 2 minutes.
  5. Add the parsley and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Toss everything together and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
