Seafood Pasta

This is a delicious seafood pasta recipe that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta
  • 1/2 pound of crab meat
  • 1/4 pound of scallops
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of halved bell tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped mushroom
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/8 cup of chili flakes
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and chili flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the chopped bell tomatoes until they become soft, about 4-5 minutes.
  5. Add the crab meat and scallops and cook until they are cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the white wine and cook until reduced by half, about 2 minutes.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the seafood.
  8. Add the basil and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Toss everything together and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
